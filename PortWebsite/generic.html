Expereince

My professional experiences are what have truly led me down the path to becoming a Data Analyst, while refining my carrer trajectory the Public Allies Central Florida program has provided me with hands on professional experience that is the foundation to growing in my career and educational goals. As a participant in this program I was fortunate enough to be partnered with Embrace Families Solutions where I work in the Pathways to Home Division as the Data and Enrollment Coordinator. In this role is where I was introduced to working with large data sets tracking thousands of our clients records and producing montlhy and qaurterly program reports. To make these processes more effeicient and visually appealing I utilized advanced Excel, SQL, and Tableau skills that made our stakeholders more engaged and involved in tracking our monlthy performances.

Prior to my stint at Embrace Families I worked for the Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission as the Activities Coordinator and an Education Specialist at the Everglades Youth Camp. These experiences allowed me to get more in touch with myself as a leader and an instructor, and challenged me to become a more organized person and quick decision maker. These are skills that I value to this day as being able to navigate a fast paced and constantly changing work environment is essential to my success up unto this point.
